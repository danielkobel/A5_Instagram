A5 Instagram Strategy

This document outlines the strategy for Atelier 5's Instagram presence.

Goals

  • Increase brand awareness
  • Engage with the audience
  • Drive traffic to the website

Content Plan

We will post a mix of content types including:

  1. Images of our work
  2. Behind-the-scenes videos
  3. Client testimonials

Posting Schedule

Posts will be scheduled as follows:

  • 3 times a week on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays

Engagement Strategy

Engagement will be fostered through:

  • Responding to comments
  • Hosting Q&A sessions
  • Collaborations with influencers
